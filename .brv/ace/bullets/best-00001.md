Multi-format JUCE plugin builds: Always use conditional compilation for format-specific macros. Example: AU-specific macros like kAudioUnitType_MIDIProcessor must be wrapped in if(APPLE) to prevent conflicts with VST3/CLAP builds. Keep format-specific dependencies isolated.
