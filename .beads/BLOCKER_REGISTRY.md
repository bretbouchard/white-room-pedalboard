### Risk Indicators

**Early Warning Signs**:
- Velocity dropping below 3 tasks/day for 2+ days
- Condition health turning yellow
- Multiple tasks in "In Progress" for >3 days
- Owner unavailable or overcommitted

### Prevention Measures

1. **Daily Monitoring**
   - Check health scores
   - Review velocity
   - Identify at-risk tasks

2. **Proactive Communication**
   - Daily standups
   - Regular check-ins
   - Early escalation

3. **Resource Management**
   - Balance workload
   - Cross-training
   - Backup assignments
